Maya 3D Laptop Model

Project Goal:

This was my finals assignment for a 3D design class where we were tasked with creating a detailed model of a real-world object and (time permitting) include a setting for the model for extra credit. So, I tasked myself with re-creating the laptop I was creating the model on, in Maya.

Creation Process:

Starting with a square cube the same dimensions as my laptop, and carving it down to include all the detail, I was able to model the laptop with all the keys, port, buttons, and vents completely to scale. After pending far too long with a ruler for my own good I then textured the model to be color accurate and show a Windows10 desktop. Despite the laptops complexity the office room was actually much more rushed together and has very little detail to it, it just serves as a backdrop.
